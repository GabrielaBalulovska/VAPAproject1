V. A. P. A

SCROLL

Voice analysis and personal assistant.

The idea of this website is to introduce a new app that people can use exclusively for sending out voice recorded messages to people on all social media platforms chosen. The app allows you to connect your Facebook, instagram, email, and phone text message, to the app and use it to send a quick message through voice analysis. When sending a message you first state to the app where you want a message sent on ie. “Facebook, Send message to (Contact), and state what you want to say. The app will also be able to identify specifically what contact you want to message given that there is more than one with the same name. Wifi or 3g will need to be enables in order for V.A.P.E to be able to message individuals on internet platforms.

The Direction

V.A.P.A is designed to ensure users have an option that is more efficient and easy for when it comes to messaging others. Voice recording yourself to either send a recorded or typed out a message is much quicker, which can be beneficial when you're on the go but need to contact someone by message at that point in time. It’s designed to do the work for you as the app goes to whatever platform you need and records for you while you’re able to continue what you may be doing instead of stopping everything you may be doing to type out a whole message to someone. V.A.P.A is useful for anyone as it can be used due to being too busy to simply send a message or just for everyday use as it is straightforward and easy to use.

Target

The age target for V.A.P.A is 16-30-year-olds, the app is an elegant design that is appealing for young teenagers and young professionals. This age group would find this app more engaging because they are commonly at the age where texting and messaging on social media is a more frequent interest. Nonetheless, adults older than 30 years old would likely find use in V.A.P.A, as it is a more simple alternative to physically messaging after all technology is becoming more complex and this app would be favourable for those who need assistance.
